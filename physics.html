Density Functional Theory Summary

For a separate project, I summarised various aspects of a scientific field known as 'Density functional Theory'. It's basically a way of understanding the properties of various materials or molecules by using a computer model of the system. It has numerous applications in a range of scientific fields. Such as improving the efficiency of solar cells by understanding how water behaves on the surface. It has extensive applications in medicine, for example better understanding how drugs bind to the HIV−1 PR virus.
